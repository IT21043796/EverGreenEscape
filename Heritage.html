Galle

Discover the beauty and history of this charming city.

Galle Dutch Fort


Explore Galle's UNESCO World Heritage site, a testament to Dutch colonial architecture and rich history.

Dutch Reformed Church

Admire 18th-century craftsmanship and rich history in the Dutch Reformed Church, a symbol of Galle's past.

The National Museum of Galle

The National Museum of Galle allows you to journey through Sri Lanka's history, showcasing artifacts from the 4th century onwards.

Sea Turtle Hatchery

A heartwarming Galle attraction dedicated to rescuing, breeding, and protecting sea turtles. A must-visit for a unique conservation experience.

Jungle Beach


Jungle Beach offers a tranquil escape with clear waters, making it an ideal spot for relaxation and snorkeling.

Japanese Peace Pagoda

Find inner peace at the Japanese Peace Pagoda, offering stunning views of the ocean and lush jungles. Attend the evening ritual for a mesmerizing experience.


Galle Lighthouse


Visit Sri Lanka's oldest lighthouse with a rich maritime history. Climb to the top for breathtaking views of the coast.

Sinharaja Forest Reserve

Declared as a World Heritage Site by UNESCO, About 95 per cent of Sri Lankan endemic birds and more than 50 per cent of endemic mammals are known to have their habitats in this Forest Reserve.

Maritime Museum


Dive into maritime history at the Maritime Museum, showcasing Dutch trading routes, nautical relics, and historical artifacts.

Yatagala Raja Maha Viharaya

Visit Yatagala Raja Maha Viharaya, an ancient Buddhist stupa adorned with elephant sculptures and surrounded by serene gardens. It's a place for contemplation and spiritual connection.
